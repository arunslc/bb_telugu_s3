Rahul Sipligunj -Singer

Rahul Sipligunj is a Telugu singer, songwriter, and musician. He shot to fame due to his Hydrabadi folk song. In 2019, he participated and won the popular reality TV show Bigg Boss Telugu (Season 3), which is hosted by Akkineni Nagarjuna.

Sreemukhi – actress

Sreemukhi Is An Indian Television Host And Actress She Was Born On 10 May 1993 In Nizamabad, Telangana, India. She Belongs To An Indian Family, And Her Zodiac Sign Is Taurus. Sreemukhi Sreemukhi Loves Dancing, Singing, Doing Yoga And Listening To Music.Sreemukhi Also Took Part In Bigg Boss Telugu 3 As A Contestant.

Baba Bhaskar – Choreographer

Baba Bhaskar Is an famous South Indian Choreographer, Writer and Director. He worked as Dance Choreographer for many Tamil Films, Telugu Films, Kannada Films and Malayalam Films. He Is one of the Reality Show Bigg Boss Telugu Season 3 Contestant.
